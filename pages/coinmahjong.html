Mahjong Game

Smooth online mahjong experience designed for fast room selection, readable gameplay, and playful daily engagement across devices.

Designing a lighter way into online mahjong

Transforms a complex tabletop game into a friendly digital experience with warm 3D room cards, large touch targets, and clear mode choices, helping players feel oriented before entering a game. The interface balances quick casual play with clear structure for room types, tournaments, friends, progress rewards, and live table info.

Readable gameplay at table scale

The in-game layout keeps the mahjong table visible first, with player status, auto-pass controls, and action states grouped around the board. The goal was to keep the table feeling alive without making the interface noisy.

Tablet experience for a complete table view

For larger screens, the interface expands into a full table composition. The tablet view gives the board more breathing room and makes the player list, settings, and live table state easier to scan during longer games.
