Best Wallet

Best Wallet is a next-generation, non-custodial crypto wallet designed to simplify access to Web3 for both newcomers and experienced crypto users.

Sept 2023

Overview

Crypto wallets tend to force a trade-off between security and usability — powerful for experts, intimidating for everyone else. The goal was to make self-custody approachable without hiding the control that experienced users expect.

As the Lead Product Designer, I was responsible for designing the end-to-end product experience across the marketing website, wallet onboarding, presale ecosystem, and Web3 payment flows.

  • Balancing the complexity of blockchain technology with an intuitive experience that still supports rapid growth and conversion.
  • Building a scalable design system for a rapidly evolving, multi-product platform.

My Role

As Lead Product Designer, I owned the end-to-end design process. My responsibilities spanned strategy through delivery:

  • Product Strategy, UX Design, and Information Architecture
  • UI Design, Motion Design, and Design System
  • Responsive Design and Developer Handoff
